About
Developed by Rodolfo Lara using GPT-4.1 nano from OpenAI and a serverless service on Azure to process responses.

Privacy & Data Use
This chat temporarily stores your messages in your browser and only sends the most recent turns to a cloud function to generate the response through the OpenAI API. When you reload or close the page, any data is erased from your browser.

We do not access your conversations. We do not store your data, and processing is performed anonymously.

OpenAI processes requests solely to generate responses and, according to their own policy, may retain them for a limited period for security purposes. We do not share your information with other third parties.
